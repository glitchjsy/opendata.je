# Privacy Policy  
Last updated: 21/11/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **opendata.je** (“we”, “our”, “the service”) collects, uses, and stores your information when you use our website and API.

## 1. Information We Collect  

### 1.1 Website Analytics  
We use **Google Analytics** to track website usage, such as page views and general interaction patterns. Google Analytics may set its own cookies, and your use of the site is subject to Google’s own privacy policies and terms.

### 1.2 API Usage Data  
When you use our API, we may collect the following information:

- **Anonymous request data**:
  - IP address
  - User agent
  - HTTP method
  - Request path  


- **Authenticated request data**:  
  If you use an API token, the request will also be associated with the **user account** linked to that token.

This information is used for security, rate limiting, debugging, and service improvement.

### 1.3 Account Information  
If you choose to create an account, we store:
- Email address
- Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)

### 1.4 Cookies  
- When you sign in, we set a cookie named **`JSESSIONID`**, which contains your session ID and is required to authenticate your login.  
- If you **do not** sign in, we do **not** set any cookies ourselves.  
- Google Analytics may set its own cookies as part of their tracking; these are governed by Google’s policies.

## 2. How We Use Your Information  
We use collected information to:

- Operate and maintain the service  
- Authenticate users  
- Monitor system health and prevent abuse  
- Improve API performance and reliability  
- Contact you regarding issues with your account (e.g., excessive usage) or significant API changes that may impact your applications

We **do not** send marketing emails, newsletters, or spam.

## 3. Sharing of Information  
We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to maintain the operation and security of the service.

Google Analytics may collect data in accordance with Google’s own privacy policy.

## 4. Data Security  
We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including hashing passwords and using secure infrastructure. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights  
You may request deletion of your account or any personal data we store about you. Contact us using the details provided on the website.

## 6. Changes to This Policy  
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
