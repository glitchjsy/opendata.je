# FOI Authors

Returns a list of all distinct *authors* of Freedom of Information requests.

```jsx
GET /v1/foi-requests/authors
```

### Response

This is a sample response.

```json title="Status: 200 (OK)"
{
  "results": [
    "States of Jersey",
    "Government of Jersey on behalf of the States Greffe",
    "Government of Jersey",
    "Stateas of Jersey",
    "States od Jersey",
    "Staes of Jersey",
    "Government of Jersey (on behalf of States of Jersey Police)",
    "Government of Jersey on behalf of the States of Jersey Police",
    "Government of Jersey on behalf of the Judicial Greffe",
    "Governmenty of Jersey"
  ]
}
```
